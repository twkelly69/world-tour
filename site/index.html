Travel tips

Pack light, roam far

These quick tips keep the itinerary flexible and stress-free.

  • Two bags max. A cabin-size roller and a daypack keep you nimble on trains and ferries.
  • Download offline maps. Mark cafes, viewpoints, and pharmacies before you land.
  • Early dinners, late sunsets. Book restaurants before dusk and save golden hour for photos.
  • Stay central. Choose neighborhoods with easy transit: Baixa, Gion, Roma Norte, and Miðborg are great bases.
  • Rest days matter. Every third day, plan nothing but a slow breakfast and a neighborhood walk.

Logistics

Weather shifts, currencies, and transit can change by the hour—here are quick checks to keep handy.

Best seasons

Apr–Jun & Sep–Nov

Avoid peak heat in Marrakesh and tropical storms in Mexico.

eSIM friendly

Airalo, Holafly, and carriers at each airport support eSIM activation.

Transit apps

Citymapper & Google Maps

Payment
